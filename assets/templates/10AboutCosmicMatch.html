About Cosmic Match

Cosmic Match is a unique program that goes beyond matching just zodiac signs, but also includes all nine planets of each person, giving the user a much better undersatanding of their relationship. It is based on each person's exact birth date, time and location. The resulting comparison gives the likelihood of relationship success on a scale of zero to 100%.

Use it with all your relationships! You can add as many matches as you like! Enjoy!
